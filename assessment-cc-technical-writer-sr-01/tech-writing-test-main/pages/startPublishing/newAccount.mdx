# Create an account

Let's create yours on Ghost:

- Go to [ghost.org](https://ghost.org/) and click the "Get strted - free" to begin.

![Image](../../public/img/logo.png)

- Fill the form with your information and create a password
- Create a title for your account. 
- Enter your banking details. These will be used to verify your identity. This ensures you a free account for 14 days, which you can cancel at any time.
